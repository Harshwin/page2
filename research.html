Interests

  • Multiphase flows, Atomization and sprays.

  • Interface-capturing algorithms, Two-fluid modeling and Multiscale methods.

  • Computational Fluid Dynamics (CFD).

  • Parallel Programming and Solver Development.

  • Turbulent Flows.



Research Projects

Developement of a parallel adaptive solver. Atomization of a liquid jet in crossflow and Secondary breakup of drops. Development of bubble coalescence and breakup models for Euler-Euler Two-fluid modeling. Direct numerical simulation of gas-liquid multiphase flows - Development of a solver. Implementation of multiphase flow models in OpenFOAM. Numerical simulation of mixed convection flow of nanofluids in a lid-driven cavity.
